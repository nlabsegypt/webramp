Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg is an American computer programmer and Internet entrepreneur. He is best known for co-founding Facebook, Inc. and serving as its chairman, chief executive officer, and controlling shareholder.

Mark was born on May 14, 1984, in White Plains, New York, and was raised in nearby Dobbs Ferry. He developed an interest in programming at an early age and went on to study at Harvard University.

In 2004, Mark launched "The Facebook" from his Harvard dormitory room. It quickly grew to become the world's largest social networking platform.
